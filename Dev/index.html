Carver Grange of Omaha (CarGO)

The George Washington Carver Grange of Omaha was established with the primary purpose to expand hands-on STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics) education and develop transferable leadership skills in youth while cultivating an interest in food and related agriculture education opportunities and careers..

Come join us! Help us strengthen our community by educating our youth about urban agriculture, urban farming or urban gardening.
