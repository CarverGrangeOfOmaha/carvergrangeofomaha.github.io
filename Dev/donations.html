What my donations will be going towards...


4H Club
Our goal is to introduce youth ages 8-12 years to integrated hands on applied Science, Technology, Engineering, Math (STEM) focused program in an out-of-school experience to help facilitate the education and development of transferable leadership skills in youth while cultivating an interest in food and agriculture.

Shadow Camps
Participating in Shadow Camp gives youth an opportunity to shadow a farm family for a day. Activities follow the farming cycle and change with the season, making each visit unique. The youth can meet the animals in the barn, help work in the fields and gardens, and see what’s cooking in the farmhouse.

Youth Roundups
We give opportunities to children and young people to learn leadership skills and then to put those skills into practical application within the Grange.Your generous donation will be used by the Junior and Youth Departments to build leaders for our Community Grange.
