4-H Club

4-H Club

Program Goal:

Our goal is to introduce youth ages 8-12 years to integrated hands–on applied Science, Technology, Engineering, Math (STEM) focused program in an out-of-school experience to help facilitate the education and development of transferable leadership skills in youth while cultivating an interest in food and agriculture.

Objectives and Activities:

  1. Expose youth to basic STEM principles
  2. Develop and share STEM educational tools to improve transferable STEM skills development
  3. Cultivate a cadre of CarGO junior leaders in agriculture.

Learning Outcomes:

  1. A minimal of eight to ten youth will participate in pilot program
  2. Participants will engage in applied STEM education and training
  3. Participants will develop a STEM project portfolio
  4. Participants will become Grange members to enhance leadership skill development.
